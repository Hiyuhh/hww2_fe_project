Frequently Asked Questions

To create a new task, simply navigate to the tasks page and click on the "New Task" button. Fill in the required details such as task name, description, and due date, then click on the "Create" button to save the task.

You can mark a task as complete by clicking on "Mark Complete" next to the task name in the Dashboard. This action will automatically update the task status to "Completed".

You can view completed tasks by accessing the Details page. There you can view all your completed and incomplete tasks.
